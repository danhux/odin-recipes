Sausage Balls For Any Time Of Year.

Sausage balls are a great snack or appetizer, we like to make them year round and serve them with honey mustard, hot mustard or BBQ sause.

Making sausage balls only takes a few minutes and they last for several days in the refrigerator.


The Ingredients You'll Need.

  • 1 Pound Of Ground Sausage
  • 3 Cups Of Baking Mix Or Bisquick
  • 3 tbl Spoons Of Hot Sauce
  • 16 oz Of Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese

Combine All Ingredients.

  1. Prehead oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mix all ingredients together by hand or using a mixer if you have one.
  3. Form the sausage mixture to the size of golf balls and place in rows on baking sheet.
  4. Bake until brown or 25 minutes

Serve warm with honey mustard, hot mustard, ranch dressing or BBQ sauce.
